# 1. Introduction

The Product Requirements Document (PRD) defines the functional scope, product objectives, business goals, and major modules of Gym ERP.

It serves as the primary reference for planning, designing, developing, testing, and maintaining the software.

Every module implemented in the system must satisfy the requirements defined within this document.

---

# 2. Product Overview

Gym ERP is a commercial-grade Gym Management System designed to simplify daily gym operations through a modern, intuitive, and highly customizable platform.

The system enables gym owners to efficiently manage members, trainers, attendance, memberships, equipment, diet plans, payments, reports, and other operational activities from a single application.

The product is intended for commercial deployment and should be adaptable to different gyms without requiring custom development.

---

# 3. Product Objectives

The primary objectives of Gym ERP are:

* Simplify gym management.
* Reduce manual administrative work.
* Organize all operational records.
* Improve staff productivity.
* Deliver a premium user experience.
* Support business customization.
* Create a reliable commercial software product.

---

# 4. Target Users

The system is intended for:

* Gym Owners
* Reception Staff
* Trainers
* Administrative Staff

Future releases may support gym members through a dedicated portal or mobile application.

---

# 5. Product Characteristics

Gym ERP should be:

* Easy to learn

* Highly customizable
* Visually outstanding
* Fast and responsive
* Secure
* Reliable
* Scalable
* Commercially deployable

---

# 6. Core Modules

Version 1.0 will include the following major modules:

### Dashboard

Provides a complete overview of daily business activities and important statistics.

---

### Member Management

Manage member registration, profiles, attendance history, membership history, payment history, assigned diet plans, invoices, notes, and activity timeline.

Member registration should remain simple and require only essential information.

---

### Trainer Management

Manage trainer profiles and records.

Required information includes:

* Trainer ID
* Full Name
* Phone Number
* Joining Date
* Years of Experience
* Specialization
* Employment Status

Optional information includes:

* Profile Photo
* Email
* Address
* Certifications
* Emergency Contact
* Notes

---

### Attendance Management

Maintain complete attendance records.

Features include:

* Check-in
* Check-out
* Daily Attendance
* Weekly Attendance
* Monthly Attendance
* Yearly Attendance
* Lifetime Attendance
* Attendance Statistics

---

### Membership Plans

Support unlimited customizable membership plans.

The system should include default plan templates while allowing gyms to modify or create plans according to their business requirements.

---

### Payment Management

Manage:

* Membership Payments
* Payment History
* Pending Payments
* Discounts
* Receipts
* Invoice Generation

---

### Equipment Management

The system should include a built-in equipment library organized by categories.

During setup or later configuration, gym owners can select the equipment available in their gym.

Owners should also be able to add completely custom equipment.

Each equipment record should support maintenance information, warranty details, status, images, and notes.

---

### Diet Plan Management

The system should include a built-in collection of diet plans.

Gym owners and trainers should be able to modify existing plans or create unlimited custom plans.

Diet plans should be assigned to members after registration from the member profile.

---

### Reports

Generate professional reports including:

* Active Members
* Expired Memberships
* Attendance
* Revenue
* Payments
* Equipment

Reports should support export functionality.

---

### Settings

Allow complete customization of business information and application settings.

Examples include:

* Gym Name
* Logo
* Contact Information
* Theme Colors
* Membership Preferences
* Business Preferences

---
