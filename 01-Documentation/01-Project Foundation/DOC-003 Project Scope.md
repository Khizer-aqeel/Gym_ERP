# 1. Purpose

This document defines the boundaries of Gym ERP Version 1.0 by clearly specifying what is included in the project and what is excluded. It ensures that development remains focused on delivering a complete, high-quality commercial product.

---

# 2. Project Scope

Gym ERP Version 1.0 will provide a complete business management solution for gyms through a modern, intuitive, and highly customizable desktop application.

The system will enable gym owners to efficiently manage members, trainers, memberships, attendance, equipment, diet plans, payments, reports, and business settings from a single platform.

---

# 3. In Scope

The following features are included in Version 1.0:

## Dashboard

* Business overview
* Key statistics
* Recent activities
* Quick actions

## Member Management

* Member registration
* Unique Member ID generation
* Member search
* Member profile
* Membership history
* Attendance history
* Payment history
* Notes

## Trainer Management

* Trainer registration
* Trainer profiles
* Years of experience (mandatory)
* Specialization
* Trainer status

## Attendance Management

* Member check-in
* Attendance history
* Daily, weekly, monthly, and yearly attendance reports

## Membership Plans

* Unlimited customizable membership plans
* Membership renewal
* Membership expiry tracking

## Payments

* Payment recording
* Payment history
* Invoice generation
* Receipt generation
* Pending payment tracking

## Equipment Management

* Built-in equipment library
* Equipment categorization
* Custom equipment support
* Equipment status
* Maintenance records

## Diet Plan Management

* Built-in diet plans
* Custom diet plans
* Assign diet plans to members

## Reports

* Member reports
* Attendance reports
* Revenue reports
* Payment reports
* Equipment reports

## Settings

* Gym information
* Branding
* Theme customization
* System preferences

---

# 4. Out of Scope

The following features are intentionally excluded from Version 1.0:

* Mobile applications
* Multi-branch management
* Online member portal
* Trainer portal
* Online payment gateway integration
* Biometric attendance devices
* AI-powered analytics
* Third-party integrations
* Cloud synchronization
* SMS and Email automation

These features may be considered for future releases.

---

# 5. Project Constraints

* The system shall be developed using free and open-source technologies.
* The software shall not require paid licenses for development.
* The user interface shall prioritize simplicity without sacrificing functionality.
* The architecture shall support future expansion.

---

# 6. Deliverables

Version 1.0 shall deliver:

* Complete desktop application
* Professional user interface
* Structured database
* Documentation
* Source code
* Installation guide
* User manual

---

# 7. Acceptance Criteria

Gym ERP Version 1.0 will be considered complete when:

* All planned modules are implemented.
* All documented requirements are satisfied.
* Critical defects are resolved.
* The application is stable for production use.
* Documentation is complete.
* The software is ready for commercial demonstrations.

---

# 8. Conclusion

This Project Scope establishes the boundaries for Gym ERP Version 1.0. Any feature not explicitly included in this document shall be considered outside the scope of Version 1.0 unless formally approved for inclusion.
